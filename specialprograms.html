Student Leadership Programs

All Programs

EOPS/CARE

EOPS offers financial assistance, counseling, tutoring, and instructional support, along with help with registration, financial aid, and university transfer. Additional services include childcare, transportation, CARE support for eligible single parents, enrichment activities, and priority registration.

CalWorks

The CalWORKs program supports eligible student parents through education, career pathways, and coordinated services that promote employment and self-sufficiency.

NextUp

NextUp provides current and former foster youth with educational, career, and personalized support services from application through graduation or transfer.

Black Cultural Resource Center

The Black Cultural Resource Center (BCRC) provides culturally responsive support, programs, and a safe, empowering community for Black students at Las Positas College.

Umoja

Umoja is a culturally centered learning community that supports student success through cohort-based courses and comprehensive academic support.

Movement API

MOVEMENT API is the Asian American, Native Hawaiian, and Pacific Islander learning community that supports students through culturally relevant curriculum, cohort classes, and personalized counseling and transfer planning.

PUENTE

The Puente Math and Science Project is a learning community that increases transfer success through integrated support in English, math, counseling, and mentoring.

Cultural Community Center

The Cultural Community Center promotes belonging and student success by offering inclusive spaces, culturally focused programs, and support for diverse student identities.

Student Life & Leadership

Student Life fosters an inclusive campus community through leadership development, student governance, and engagement opportunities beyond the classroom.

Basic Needs

The Basic Needs program supports student well-being by reducing food insecurity and other basic needs barriers to academic success.
